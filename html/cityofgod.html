City of God

City of God (Portuguese: Cidade de Deus) is a 2002 epic crime film directed by Fernando Meirelles and co-directed by Katia Lund.

The film begins in medias res with an armed gang chasing after an escaped chicken in a favela called the Cidade de Deus ("City of God"). The chicken stops between the gang and the narrator, a young man nicknamed Rocket ("Buscape").
